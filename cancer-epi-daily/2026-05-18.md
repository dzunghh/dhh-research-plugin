- **Journal**: Nature Reviews Clinical Oncology
- **Pub date**: 2026-04-27 (online-first; not in prior digest corpus)
- **Authors**: Lee DJ et al.
- **Aims**: Synthesise global colorectal cancer incidence patterns and the rising early-onset CRC burden across geographic regions.
- **Method**: Narrative review synthesising GLOBOCAN registry data and age–period–cohort analyses across 185 countries.
- **Key findings**: CRC incidence is rising globally beyond historically high-burden Western regions, driven primarily by early-onset CRC (<50 years). Dietary patterns, gut microbiota composition, and environmental contaminants are implicated as key drivers.
